Welcome to Gwalior Pride School

Pathways School Gurgaon was the first ever IB Continuum school in India offering all 4 programmes of the International Baccalaureate Organisation (IBO) of Geneva, Switzerland.

The school focuses on student-centered learning and applies the Multiple Intelligences approach, developed by Dr. Howard Gardner from Harvard University.
